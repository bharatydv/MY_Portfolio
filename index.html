    Sentiment Analysis of the COVID-19 Pandemic Tweets

    Published on IJRAR.ORG

    The sentiment analysis of the COVID-19 pandemic, as published on IJRAR.ORG, leverages cutting-edge AI and ML methodologies to gain insights into public sentiment during this global crisis. By harnessing the power of Artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) algorithms, researchers analyze a vast corpus of data, including social media posts, news articles, and online forums, to discern prevailing sentiments.

    The findings of this sentiment analysis provide a comprehensive understanding of how individuals and communities perceive and respond to the COVID-19 pandemic. By uncovering patterns and trends in sentiment, researchers can identify areas of concern, assess the effectiveness of public health measures, and inform decision-making processes.
